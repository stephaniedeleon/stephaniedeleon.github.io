The Story_


Hello, world! I’m Stephanie, a second-year Computer Science major and an aspiring VIP (Very Inspiring Person).

My focus is simple: live a vibrant and fulfilling life while being an inspiration to others!

With my passion for Software Engineering, I developed Love, Panini from the ground up as a medium to share my passions, stories, and lessons with those willing to read or listen.

With my words, I hope to encourage and guide people to a better life and lifestyle. With my recipes, I hope to make people happy and satisfied. With my tech projects, I hope to spark interest in CS and promote diversity in tech. I may not be the best writer or developer, but hopefully, my sincerity seeps through.

Why Love, Panini?_


The nickname “Panini” originated from my Kuya (an older cousin), and it has been around for as long as I can remember. The name sits very close to my heart because (1) it’s uniquely cute, (2) for me, it’s very endearing, and (3) I get to be an Italian food!

When people write letters, they usually end their letters with a closing and a signature, and I wanted to do something similar. But instead of letters, it would be this website! As I would in a heartfelt letter, I will create content and develop projects with love and care!

Hence the name - Love, Panini.

What will I find in Love, Panini?_


Plenty of stuff! Ranging from wellness advice to life lessons. From tech projects to recipes and more! And most importantly, you would be able to embark on this journey with me as I learn more about life!

At the end of each blog, there are small code snippets - have a look! They are meant to be understood by everyone!

Thanks for taking the time to read my story, and I hope you enjoy my website!
