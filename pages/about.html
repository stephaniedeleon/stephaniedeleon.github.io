The Story_


I’m Stephanie, a second-year Computer Science major & an aspiring VIP (Very Inspiring Person); and Panini is my family nickname and is very dear to me.

When people write letters they usually end their letters with a closing and a signature, and I wanted to do the same thing. But instead of letters it would be this website! As I would in a heartfelt letter, I will be creating content and developing projects with love, care, and passion.

Hence the name - Love, Panini

Love, Panini_


I’ve always aspired to inspire people and share my passions to those who are willing to listen, and I wanted to achieve this with my fondness for Software Engineering.

So, I decided to take on Web Development and code a website from the ground up where I can comfortably share my stories and experiences to the world.

With my words, I hope to inspire and guide people into a more fulfilling life. With my recipes, I hope to make people happy and satisfied. With my tech projects, I hope to inspire underrepresented groups to not be afraid to pursue a career in Engineering. I may not be the best writer but hopefully, my sincerity seeps through.

At the end of each blog, there are small code snippets - have a look! They are meant to be understood by everyone!

Thanks for taking the time to read and I hope you enjoy my website!
